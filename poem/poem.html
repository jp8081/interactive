The sound of a mountain

Imbues me with curiosity

A sound my ears know

But do not often hear


How can it be

That a mountain

So earthly

And familiar


Is a place I will never summit

A place that by all measures

Is as distant as space itself

A place

Whose sound I only hear

in my dreams


I feel an obligation

To realize this dream

To summit this mountain


I try to summit mountains every day

To hear their sound

Of freedom

And every day

As I reach the top

I am deafened

By the relentless ringing

Of work that must get done

Go figure


This is my struggle

instruction can not help

Because I don’t think

My instructors

Have heard this sound either


I ask my mother

How she copes

With the ringing

Her responses never change

“I try not to think about it”


I need to get away
