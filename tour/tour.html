my living room

Our couch - Has a permanent red protective cover on because the dogs love to use it as home base when chasing each other. Featuring my dad passed out probably dreaming about moving off-grid and building a cabin.

Our fireplace. This hasn't been used... ever? There are chimney swifts living in the chimney now, so we'll never use it.

The ottoman - Catch my dad sleeping here practically any hour of the day. Has a permanently indented cushion. It's a rare occurance actually that he's on the couch today.

The dog fence we use when guests are over. Josie figured out how to jump over it but Katie is still clueless.

The dog window - has a perfect view of our driveway so whenever you come home you can always see a dog's face looking at you from this window.

Our living room rug. This thing will probably outlive me. I can't imagine a living room not having a rug that looks like this.

The TV - currently obstructed by a workout bench which I used during Covid times. Somehow no one has moved it yet.
